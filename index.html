Xixuan Hao

I'm a PhD student at HKUST(GZ), supervised by Prof. Yuxuan Liang and Prof. Yangqiu Song.

Before that, I received my Master's degree from HKU. I was a research intern in CUHK(SZ) in 2023, focusing on Medical AI, working with Prof. Ruimao Zhang. I was a research intern in Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA) in 2022, working with Dr. Danqing Huang and Prof. Chin-Yew Lin, focusing on UI Object Detection. I was a research intern in The Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology (SIAT), Chinese Academy of Science in 2021, working with Dr. Bin Fu and Prof. Yu Qiao, focusing on Scene Text Spotting (OCR).

I'm currently interested in Urban Computing, Urban Representation Learning, Multimodal Learning and Spatio-Temporal Data Mining.
